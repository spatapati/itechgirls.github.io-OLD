  • About Us

For Kids. Built By Kids.

Technology is omnipresent everywhere one goes in this ever fast paced twenty-first century. We often pride ourself as being the new and diverse modern age, but a clear contrast to this statement can be seen by the prominent gender that exists between men in women in the computer science and technology fields. Inspired and motivated to power young women harness the ultimate super power of coding, two highschool students teamed up to show the world that gender by no means serves as a barrier to what one can accomplish.
