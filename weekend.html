Weekend Program

Learn To Create A Dyanmic And Functional Web Application

About The Course

The weekend program (which runs for five conseceutive Saturdays)will provide its students with all the skills necessary to create a dynamic, interactive and functional web application, as well as provide the students with an outlined plan regarding how they will be able to continue learning computer science, even after the final camp session. During the program the students will become proficient in HTML and CSS, and will be able to use Twitter Bootstrap to enhance the quality of their web application. If time permits, we may be able to introduce the girls to a more complex back-end programming language, such as Javascript, as well, and the afterschool program will have a 1:3 teacher to student ratio.

Location

Named a 2015 National Blue Ribbon School by the U.S. Department of Education and recognized as the #5 School in the Nation by Newsweek 2015, the Bergen County Academies (BCA), is a free public magnet high school of choice for Bergen County residents located on the Dr. John Grieco Campus in Hackensack, New Jersey. The school offers seven specialized programs in the areas of mathematics and science, engineering and design, medical science, business and finance, culinary arts and hospitality administration, visual and performing arts, and technology and computer science.

Session One

This year ITechGirls will be hosting a weekend program as an alternative to the afterschool program, in order to be able to cater to those who may be unavailable to attend programs on weekdays, and thereby expanding our audience to target a wider community of girls who want to code. Our weekend programs will run on four conseceutive Saturdays, and will last from 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM each day. The program will start on Saturday, March 5th and end on Saturday, March 26th.
